{% call heading3('data-collected') %} What data do we collect? {% endcall %}

The data we collect are those that you provide us when you register in our site through OAuth2 Providers (Google, Discord, Github). These data might include, but not be limited to, your username, your email, your avatar, your OAuth2 Provider, and your OAuth2 Provider's ID.

Additionally, public network information such as browsers, IP address for security purpose are collected.

{% call heading3('data-usage') %} How do we use your data? {% endcall %}

For OAuth2-provided information, we use them to identify you and provide protected resources according to your resources. For example, we need your login handler (provided by an OAuth2 Provider of your choice) to create a user account and resources associated with this account.

For network information such as web browsers and IP address, we use them to identify your device and provide security protection. For example, we monitor your IP address to prevent rate-limiting.

{% call heading3('how-we-collect') %} How do we collect your data? {% endcall %}

You directly provide us with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register online or use our API endpoints
  • Use or view our website
{% call heading3('how-we-store') %} How do we store your data? {% endcall %}

We securely store your data in our database. We will store your database until you request to delete your account. Once your account is deleted, your data will be removed from our database.

{% call heading3('who-shares-with') %} Who do we share your data with? {% endcall %}

Our app doesn't share your data with any third-party. Your data is only for user authentication, authorization, providing protected resources, and security protection.

We do not sell any of your data nor personal information to others.
