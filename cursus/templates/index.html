A public API for universities and courses data

What this API offers

This is a public, free-to-use API that allows you to query for universities and courses information. The database provides information over 1000 universities globally.

With this API, you can query for universities, their domains, their country, their history as well as the courses they offer.

University

A variety of endpoints that allow you to find and retrieve information about universities based on institution, campuse, etc.

Departments & Courses

Search for courses and departments' information based on the name, the university, the department, etc.

Domains & Campusus

Find your university's domain and campus information for a nice and easy integration with your application.

Biography

Get to know more about the founders, and the history of your universities.
